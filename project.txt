Website: Verdict.digital
High-level goal
Build a Polymarket-style political prediction platform, but with:
- play money only (at least initially)
- virality designed into the product (not “marketing”)
- manual governance + AI assistance
- production-grade thinking within a lean $5k budget

This is a serious prototype, not a throwaway MVP.

UI / UX
- The layout and UX patterns should include the same *elements* as Polymarket because they’re proven and intuitive:
  - market cards/grid
  - categories
  - trending / popular / top movers
  - market page with chart, volume, activity, buy/sell panel
  - portfolio, leaderboard, public profiles
- Important: we will NOT copy branding, code, CSS, or market text. UX patterns are fine; cloning is not.

Market discovery (Polymarket as topic radar only)
I want to scrape Polymarket **political topics only**, used internally as a signal source.

Use scraping + AI internally to:
- detect political topics
- cluster related political events
- suggest new markets

BUT every published market must be native:
- new wording (not copied)
- new IDs
- new terms & conditions (unique per market)
- independent resolution criteria (official sources, not Polymarket)

Polymarket is an input for topic discovery, not a dependency and not mirrored.

Manual governance (non-negotiable)
Admins must be able to:
- create, edit, approve markets
- define terms & resolution criteria
- pause/freeze markets
- resolve markets manually
- override or dispute automated suggestions

Automation can propose markets and resolutions, but humans are final authority.

Trading model
- Buy / sell UX similar to Polymarket.
- CPMM + hybrid architecture is fine (and likely), but you can recommend the exact mechanism.
- Play money only.
- Instant execution with visible price movement.

Probability display (important)
- Raw market price exists internally.
- Displayed “% probability” must be stable in low-volume markets.
- Use Bayesian smoothing with a Beta prior for displayed probability.
- Explain how α/β are chosen and how the display converges as liquidity increases.

Game & virality layer (CORE REQUIREMENT)
Virality must be a side-effect of normal usage.

Required:
- Weekly fake-money faucet for all users
- Weekly leaderboards + lifetime stats
- Reputation / accuracy scoring more important than raw P&L
- Public profiles and market pages viewable without login

Sharing must be:
- one-click
- frictionless
- platform-agnostic

Users must be able to generate and share **visual prediction artifacts** across:
- Instagram Stories (9:16 story cards — critical)
- X / Twitter
- WhatsApp
- Telegram
- Reddit
- Discord
- Copyable links for private chats

Shareable artifacts include:
- “I give this a 72% chance” prediction cards
- Market probability snapshots
- Resolution outcomes (“the market called it”)
- Leaderboard rank / streak badges

Shared links must land on public pages (market or profile) with no login wall.

Resolution moments (very important)
- Market resolution should trigger:
  - notification/email
  - clear win/loss outcome
  - explanation + authoritative source
- Resolution moments should be designed to be socially shareable.

Scope discipline
- No real money
- No wallets / crypto
- No KYC
- No on-chain requirements
- Web app only

Architecture freedom
You’re free to choose:
- backend stack
- infra
- tooling
as long as the system is:
- correct
- auditable
- evolvable
- cheap to operate


I’m intentionally optimizing for virality, correctness, and speed — not over-engineering.
